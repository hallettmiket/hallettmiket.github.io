This course aims to provide students from biology and the life sciences in general (e.g. biology, cell biology, biochemistry, immunology, physiology) with instruction in advanced techniques of bioinformatics, computational biology and data science.

As it is a reading course, the syllabus can be modified to meet the interests of specific students in the class.
